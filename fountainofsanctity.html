what is BMI?

Your Body Mass Index (BMI) is an anthropometric index of weight and height, and is the commonly accepted index for classifying adiposity (the amount of fat in the body). Despite its universal popularity, it is not a good single indicator of excess fat, as further assessment is needed (such as body-fat percentage). The BMI calculation is the same across both sexes for adults, but not for children and teenagers. Please only refer to the above calculator if you are 21 or older.

BMI Classification
< 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal/Healthy Weight
25 - 29.9 Overweight
30+ Obese
